Egg Fried Rice

Description

A takeaway staple which is super easy to make and packed full of flavour. Works great as a side dish or a main course.

Ingredients

  • 300g cooked rice
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Vegetable oil
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic salt

Method

  1. Heat a little vegetable oil in a wok or large frying pan over a medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  2. Push the onion and garlic to one side of the pan and crack the eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs with a spatula until cooked through.
  3. Add the rice to the pan and stir well to combine with the onion, garlic and eggs.
  4. Season with a good splash of soy sauce, a sprinkle of garlic salt and a drizzle of sesame oil. Stir well to combine.
  5. Continue to cook for a further 5 minutes until the rice is heated through and everything is well combined.
  6. Remove from the heat and serve with a wedge of lemon.
